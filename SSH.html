How to connect VSCode to the Student Machine using SSH

Step 1

Control Shift p or click the blue button on the bottom left corner

Step 2

Enter the following command: “Remote-SSH: Add New SSH Host…”

Step 3

Click “+ Add New SSH Host”

Step 4

Enter “ssh USERNAME@student2.cs.appstate.edu”

Step 5

POTENTIAL: click “Linux”

Step 6

Press enter

Step 7

Click the “Connect” button in the bottom right corner
